Get to grips with your personal data

This is a project to visualise some of the data that companies hold about you. We've started with Google, as they seem to have the most.

The aim is to increase awareness of the data you're giving to companies. We are not suggesting it is a good or a bad thing.

Get started by clicking Download your Data and following the instructions.

This is a project I have started to learn D3.js. If you're interested in learning it too, or are already an expert and want to contribute, please do have a look at the GitHub.
