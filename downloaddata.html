Download Your Data

Downloading your Google data is pretty easy. Here goes:

  1. Go to Google Takeout
  2. Click Deselect all (there's too much there) and then select My Activity and Location History.
  3. For both, make sure you click 'Multiple formats' and select JSON.
  4. It'll take Google a while to fetch all the data, but when it's ready, head to the other pages on this site to upload and see what's there.
  5. REMEMBER: When you upload to this site, the data stays in your browser and does not leave your computer. No data is sent to a server.
