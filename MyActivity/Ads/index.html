Google Ad Data

You have access to a list of all the websites where you have ever seen an advert served to you by Google. This is a long, boring JSON file. However you can just upload here for a nice visual summary.

Instructions:

  1. Follow the instructions on the homepage to download your data from Google. You should have downloaded the My Activity selection to get Ads data.
  2. Click upload below, and select the file called MyActivity.json, in the Ads folder.
  3. Have a look at your data that will appear below. You will be able to see how many ads you see, and where you see them most.
